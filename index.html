Greeneville WALKS

Take an inspiring stroll through historic, downtown Greeneville and learn about all that nature has to offer!

ABOUT: Here you will find all sorts of information concerning the Greeneville downtown area as well as directional guides regarding several brief walking tours that originate from the Walters State Niswonger Campus. This Web site provides tools, resources, and information concerning Greeneville's past present and future such as history, nature, cuurent events, local business, local government, area national parks and civil war trails. This is a place where our community of learners can come together.
